How it Works

Capture, Reuse, and Save

The BESOS exhaust system captures the CO2 and methane emissions from your car, then reuses them to power your vehicle, saving you fuel and reducing your environmental impact.

  • Capture Emissions

    The BESOS system captures the CO2 and methane emissions from your car's exhaust.

  • Reuse the Emissions

    The captured emissions are then reused to power your vehicle, reducing fuel consumption.

  • Save Money and the Environment

    By capturing and reusing emissions, you'll save money on fuel and reduce your carbon footprint.
